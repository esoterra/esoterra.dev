Kyle Brown

I'm Kyle Brown. I am an optimistic and often idealistic Software Engineer.

I'm hopeful about the potential of technologies like WebAssembly (Wasm) and Rust. I think we can use them to build a solid foundation for the future of Software Engineering and I'm proud to work on and with them.

I think compilers are our friends, constraints are guarantees, and the fundamental problem of Software Engineering is balancing investing in the future and solving current problems.

I am also a human being (shocking but it's true) and I have a wide variety of other interests and hobbies that I have not yet but may eventually write about here too.
